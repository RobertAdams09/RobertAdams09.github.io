About Nurburgring Car Club

The main goal of the Nurburgring Car Club (NCC) is to unite car enthusiasts that enjoy driving on the Green Hell.

NCC also actively supports and advertises Nurburgring Nordshleife.

NCC provides information for people who want to come to the Nurburgring.

NCC also provides a tow truck in case of any breakdowns or accidents.

Our most skilled and experienced club members give free instruction laps.

Events and Trackdays

NCC organizes driving academies with professional instructors during which you can improve your driving skills.

We organize regular meetings and outings, so that our members can get to know each other and have a good laugh every once in a while.

How To Join The Nurburgring Car Club

You can join the NCC by:

  1. sending us an email with short description of yourself and your car (photos are more than welcome).
  2. finding us on our social media and sending us a direct message (SocialMediaLinks)
  3. visiting our small office located in Nurburg on HauptstraBe 24A.

Here you can find the answers for all your questions about the NCC membership:

1. How much do I have to pay for joining the Nurburgring Car Club?

Becoming a member of NCC is absolutely free, you do not have to pay anything. Club membership is free for lifetime, so the only costs that you have to think of are lap tickets, fuel and your car maintanance.

2. How can I resign from the NCC membership?

Although we do not want you to resign from the membership in our club, it is very easy and convienient - just send us an email asking for the cancellation of your club membership.

3. How often are the events and trackdays taking place?

We try to organize trackdays and events every month. All of the events and trackdays organized by NCC take place on weekends, so you do not have to worry about not being able to show up due to work or any other duties.

4. How often are the instruction laps organized?

Instruction laps with our most experienced drivers are a very open concept. What does it mean? If you want to go for an instruction lap just ask one of the club members to join you and they will definitely agree.

5. Are driving academies organized by the NCC free of charge?

No, if you want to take part in the driving academy organized by the NCC you have to pay since we have to rent a whole racetrack or skidtrack, and we also have to pay the instructors. Most often our driving academy costs around 900 euro.

6. How much do the trackdays cost?

Trackdays usually cost between 400 and 600 euro. It mostly depends on the number of participants and the time of the year, trackdays during summer are more expensive due to the high demand and many other events that are taking place.
