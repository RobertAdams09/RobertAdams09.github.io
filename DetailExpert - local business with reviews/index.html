Detail Expert

We pride ourselves on making our customers happy through extended and deep cleaning of their beloved cars.

If you love cars like we do at Detail Expert, that means you have come to the right place.

We offer a wide variety of ways to take care of your car, like:

  • cleaning, oxydizing and washing the exterior of your car
  • applying special protection coatings and waxes onto the car's paint and windows
  • vacuuming, cleaning and washing the interior of your car
  • protecting plastic, metal, wood and other interior elements with the usage of professional products
  • restoring and repaiting the leather seats, steering wheels, knobs and dashboards
  • and much more than that!

In Detail Expert we also offer wrapping

We can wrap interior or exterior elements in any kind of wrap design, color or pattern that you desire
and if you are really feeling in need of change we can transform your entire car's looks by wrapping it!
